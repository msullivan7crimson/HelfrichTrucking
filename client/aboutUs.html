Our Services

Helfrich Trucking offers unparalleled reliability and expertise in the industry. We provide a range of services including crane load assistance, trailer storage, and rental. Whether you need local, regional, or nationwide transportation across the lower 48 states, we have the capabilities to meet your requirements.

Primary Service Areas

With a strong presence in Illinois, Wisconsin, and Iowa, we are located just an hour from Lasalle-Peru, Chicago, Madison, and Davenport, and only 30 minutes from Rockford. Our team is committed to delivering exceptional service for all your transportation needs.

Our Fleet

Our fleet is equipped with various types of trailers to accommodate diverse hauling needs. From hotshot and stepdeck trailers to flatbed, winch machinery, agricultural slideout trailers, and 8-12 axle beam and low boy trailers, we ensure that your freight is transported safely and efficiently. We pride ourselves on our ability to handle complex and challenging hauls, providing solutions that go beyond standard transportation services.

Our Certifications

Certified Expertise: At Helfrich Trucking, safety and professionalism are paramount. Our certifications include Local 150 and BNSF E-Rail certification, ensuring that our team meets the highest industry standards. This commitment to certification guarantees that we are equipped to handle every project with exceptional safety, precision, and expertise.
