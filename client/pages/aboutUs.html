Our Services

At Helfrich Trucking & Excavating, we pride ourselves on being a leading provider of specialized transport solutions for a diverse range of industries including Construction, Railroad, Agriculture, Wind Energy, and Crane operations.

Primary Service Areas/Fleet

Based in Northern Illinois, our services extend throughout the Greater Rockford Area and the Midwest, covering all lower 48 states. Our expertise lies in handling oversized, overweight, and specialized cargo with precision and safety. With a robust fleet that includes flatbeds, stepdecks, flip tail machinery trailers, beam insert lowboys, and configurations up to 70-ton capacity on 8-12 axles lowboys, we are equipped to meet the most demanding transportation needs.
